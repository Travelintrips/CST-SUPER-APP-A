FASE 3D — MEMBERSHIP PAYMENT + ACCOUNTING

Jangan ubah promo.
Jangan ubah refund.
Jangan ubah purchase.

Fokus hanya membership payment.

TUJUAN

Membership Sport Center harus:

1. Bisa dibayar
2. Tercatat di sport_payments
3. Membentuk jurnal accounting
4. Muncul di dashboard revenue

A. AUDIT EXISTING

Periksa terlebih dahulu:

* struktur tabel sport_members
* field status membership
* field start_date
* field end_date
* field company_id

Laporkan jika ada field yang perlu ditambahkan.

B. PAYMENT ENDPOINT

Tambahkan endpoint:

POST /api/sport-center/members/:id/payment

requireAdmin

Body:

{
amount,
payment_method,
notes
}

C. VALIDASI

Pastikan:

* member ada
* member aktif
* amount > 0
* company_id tersedia

Return 400 jika gagal.

D. PAYMENT RECORD

Simpan ke sport_payments:

payment_type = membership

company_id

member_id

customer_id

amount

payment_method

notes

created_at

Jika field belum ada, buat migration yang diperlukan.

E. ACCOUNTING

Tambahkan function baru:

postSportCenterMembershipPayment()

Lokasi:

lib/accounting.ts

Source:

sport_center_membership

Jurnal:

Debit:
Kas

Credit:
Pendapatan Membership Sport Center

company_id:
ambil dari member

memo:

Membership Payment <member_number>

F. CHART OF ACCOUNT

Periksa apakah akun berikut sudah ada:

Pendapatan Membership Sport Center

Jika belum:

buat account otomatis mengikuti pola account revenue lain yang sudah ada.

Jangan mengubah akun existing.

G. DASHBOARD

Tambahkan:

membershipRevenue

membershipPayments

activeMembers

expiredMembers

H. REPORTS

Tambahkan ke laporan revenue:

booking revenue

membership revenue

grand total revenue

I. AUDIT LOG

action:

MEMBERSHIP_PAYMENT_CREATED

metadata:

member_id
amount
payment_method
