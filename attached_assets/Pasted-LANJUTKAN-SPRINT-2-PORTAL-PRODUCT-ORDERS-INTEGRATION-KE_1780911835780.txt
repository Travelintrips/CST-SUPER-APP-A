LANJUTKAN SPRINT 2 — PORTAL PRODUCT ORDERS INTEGRATION KE PROFITABILITY ENGINE.

Tujuan:
Memasukkan portal_product_orders / MCT orders ke profitability analytics, dan memastikan product_price, vendor cost, shipment_cost, truck_cost terisi agar Product Margin dan Shipment Margin mulai menghasilkan angka nyata.

Jangan ubah UI besar dulu.
Jangan ubah invoice/payment workflow.
Fokus backend analytics + data population.

Scope:

1. Schema / Data Field Audit

Audit portal_product_orders:

Pastikan field berikut ada:
- grand_total
- subtotal
- vendor_quoted_price
- shipment_cost
- truck_cost
- product_price
- origin
- destination
- commodity
- company_id
- customer_id
- vendor_id

Jika belum ada:
- tambahkan field missing secara additive
- jangan rename field lama
- jangan drop kolom lama

2. Populate Data Saat Order Dibuat / Diupdate

Pastikan saat MCT / portal product order dibuat:
- product_price terisi dari subtotal atau total harga produk
- vendor_quoted_price terisi saat vendor product quote diterima
- shipment_cost terisi saat customer pilih shipment
- truck_cost terisi jika trucking digunakan
- origin terisi dari pickup/origin
- destination terisi dari alamat tujuan
- commodity terisi dari product category / commodity
- company_id terisi

3. Profitability Engine

Update analyticsProfit.ts:

Masukkan portal_product_orders ke:

- /api/analytics/profitability/orders
- /api/analytics/profitability/customers
- /api/analytics/profitability/routes
- /api/analytics/profitability/commodities

Gunakan UNION ALL dengan logistic_orders.

Normalisasi output:

sourceType:
- logistic_order
- portal_product_order

orderNumber:
- logistic_orders.order_number
- portal_product_orders.order_number / mct_number

Formula portal product order:

Product Revenue
= product_price atau subtotal

Product Cost
= vendor_quoted_price

Shipment Revenue
= shipment_cost

Shipment Cost
= truck_cost

Product Margin
= Product Revenue - Product Cost

Shipment Margin
= Shipment Revenue - Shipment Cost

Order Gross Margin
= Product Margin + Shipment Margin

Margin %
= Order Gross Margin / grand_total * 100

4. Guard Null

Jika product_price null:
- productRevenue = subtotal

Jika vendor_quoted_price null:
- productCost = 0

Jika shipment_cost null:
- shipmentRevenue = 0

Jika truck_cost null:
- shipmentCost = 0

Jangan crash jika data lama belum lengkap.
