IMPLEMENT PRODUCT-FIRST PHASE 2B-1 — CUSTOMER PORTAL UI.

Jangan ubah backend besar.
Jangan ubah DB.
Jangan ubah shipment flow lama.

Tujuan:
Customer bisa menjalankan product-first flow dari portal.

Scope:

1. Update product-order.tsx
- Customer pilih produk dulu
- Customer submit orderType = product_first
- Shipment mode tidak wajib di awal
- Jangan otomatis forward ke shipper

2. Buat halaman baru:
/product-approve/:token

Fungsi:
- tampilkan detail produk
- vendor produk
- harga produk
- qty
- ready date
- pickup location
- dokumen produk jika ada
- tombol Approve
- tombol Reject

Approve:
POST /api/logistic/orders/:token/customer-product-approve

3. Buat halaman baru:
/shipment-selection/:token

Fungsi:
- hanya bisa dibuka jika status = Shipment Selection Pending
- tampilkan data produk confirmed
- pilihan shipment:
  - Pickup sendiri
  - Trucking
  - Air Freight
  - Sea Freight
  - Door to Door

Jika Pickup sendiri:
POST select-shipment-mode dengan shipmentMode = pickup_self

Jika memilih shipment lain:
POST select-shipment-mode dengan shipmentMode sesuai pilihan
lalu tampilkan pesan:
"Tim kami akan mengirimkan penawaran pengiriman."

4. Update tracking customer

Untuk orderType = product_first, tampilkan step:

Order Received
Admin Review
Product RFQ Sent
Product Quote Received
Product Vendor Selected
Customer Product Approval
Shipment Selection Pending
Ready for Pickup / Shipment RFQ Sent
Vendor Confirmed
In Progress
Pickup
In Transit
Arrived
Delivered
POD Uploaded
Invoice Issued
Payment Received
Completed

Untuk orderType = shipment, jangan berubah.

5. Routing
Pastikan halaman baru masuk router customer-portal.

6. Test
Gunakan E2E_TEST_MODE=true.

Test:
A. product_first order bisa dibuat dari customer portal
B. product approve page bisa dibuka dengan token valid
C. token invalid tampil error
D. approve product → status Shipment Selection Pending
E. shipment selection page tampil
F. pilih pickup_self → status Ready for Pickup
G. pilih trucking → shipmentMode tersimpan
H. shipment order lama tetap tracking normal

Output:
A. File yang diubah
B. Page baru
C. Route baru
D. Test result
E. Risk tersisa
