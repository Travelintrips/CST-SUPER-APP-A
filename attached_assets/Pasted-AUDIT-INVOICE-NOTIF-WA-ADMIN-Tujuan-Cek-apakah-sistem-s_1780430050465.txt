AUDIT INVOICE & NOTIF WA ADMIN

Tujuan:
Cek apakah sistem sudah memiliki setting invoice otomatis dan notifikasi WA admin yang siap trigger saat driver upload Bukti Pengiriman (POD).

---

A. BACKEND AUDIT

1. **Invoice Schema / Table**

   * Cek `invoices` table:

     * Field: `invoice_number`, `logisticOrderId`, `customerId`, `amount_total`, `due_date`, `status` (READY / ISSUED / PAID)
   * Cek apakah ada relasi ke `driver_jobs` / `logisticOrderId`
   * Cek template default / format PDF / HTML untuk invoice

2. **Invoice Generation Trigger**

   * Endpoint / function yang membuat invoice otomatis post-POD
   * Pastikan logika sudah men-generate invoice record
   * Catat field yang di-set: nomor invoice, amount_total, due_date, status

3. **WA Admin Notification**

   * Cek helper / service function yang dipanggil post-POD
   * Pastikan sudah include:

     * invoiceNumber
     * logisticOrderId
     * amount_total
     * link invoice
   * Pastikan fire-and-forget async, tidak block upload driver
   * Partial test → log debug WA, tanpa kirim live

4. **WA Customer Notification**

   * Cek apakah trigger notif ke customer saat invoice dibuat
   * Partial test → bisa hanya log debug

5. **Audit Logging**

   * Pastikan semua event tercatat di log: invoice generate, WA send result, driver/job info

---

B. FRONTEND AUDIT

1. **Customer Portal**

   * Cek section “Invoice” di order detail page
   * Field ditampilkan:

     * Nomor invoice
     * Tanggal / due date
     * Total harga / PPN
     * Status: READY / ISSUED
   * Tombol preview / download PDF

2. **Admin Panel**

   * Cek badge / notification muncul saat invoice otomatis dibuat
   * Link invoice ready → bisa dikirim ke customer
   * Timeline / OrderDriverAssignmentPanel menampilkan invoice linked ke job
