UPDATE LOGISTICS RFQ & CUSTOMER PORTAL — GATING TRUCK PANEL + CUSTOMER TOTAL

Tujuan:

1. Truck panel di Review Order hanya muncul setelah vendor produk disetujui (`productApproved = true`)
2. Customer Portal menampilkan total order (harga produk + truk) + label Internal / Eksternal

---

A. BACKEND

1. **Expose productApproved**

   * Endpoint GET `/review-order` / `/logistic-order/:id`
   * Sertakan field `productApproved: boolean` per RFQ

2. **Customer Portal Data**

   * Endpoint GET `/customer-order/:orderId/progress` / `/invoice-summary`
   * Sertakan:

     * `productPrice`
     * `truckPrice`
     * `truckSource` → Internal / External
     * `totalPrice = productPrice + truckPrice`

3. **Validation**

   * Truck panel hanya muncul jika backend menandai `productApproved = true`
   * Jika `productApproved = false` → frontend menampilkan pesan “Panel truk akan muncul setelah produk disetujui”

---

B. FRONTEND — Logistics RFQ Comparison Panel

1. **Truck Panel Gating**

   * Wrap panel dengan condition:

     ```ts
     {productApproved && <TruckAssignmentPanel />}
     {!productApproved && <p>Panel truk akan muncul setelah produk disetujui</p>}
     ```
2. **Live Update**

   * Setelah productApproved diubah → panel truck muncul otomatis tanpa reload

---

C. FRONTEND — Customer Portal

1. **Total Order + Label**

   * Ambil data dari backend: `productPrice + truckPrice` → total
   * Ambil `truckSource` → tampilkan badge Internal / External
2. **UI**

   * Timeline atau Invoice Card:

     ```
     Produk: Rp XXX
     Truk: Rp XXX [Internal / External]
     Total: Rp XXX
     ```
   * Update realtime jika admin assign truk atau internal/external berubah
