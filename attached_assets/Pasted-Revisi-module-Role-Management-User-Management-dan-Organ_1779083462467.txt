Revisi module Role Management, User Management, dan Organization Structure agar support Division dan Department secara penuh.

Masalah saat ini:

* Role Management belum support Division
* Role Management belum support Department
* Permission belum bisa dibatasi berdasarkan division dan department

Tolong audit dulu schema existing dan flow existing sebelum implementasi.

==================================================

1. USER ORGANIZATION STRUCTURE
   ==================================================

Setiap user wajib bisa di-assign ke:

* company_id
* branch_id
* division_id
* department_id
* section_id optional

Struktur:
Company
└── Branch
└── Division
└── Department
└── Section/Team optional
└── User

==================================================
2. DIVISION MANAGEMENT
======================

Tambahkan module Division Management.

Field:

* division_code
* division_name
* company_id
* branch_id
* manager_id
* description
* is_active

Contoh:

* Logistics Division
* Technology Division
* Finance Division
* Retail Division
* F&B Division

==================================================
3. DEPARTMENT MANAGEMENT
========================

Tambahkan module Department Management.

Field:

* department_code
* department_name
* company_id
* branch_id
* division_id
* manager_id
* description
* is_active

Contoh:

* Purchasing Department
* Warehouse Department
* Accounting Department
* Kitchen Department
* Sales Department

==================================================
4. ROLE MANAGEMENT
==================

Role wajib support scope:

* all_companies
* company_only
* branch_only
* division_only
* department_only

Role Form wajib punya:

* Company
* Branch
* Division
* Department
* Role Name
* Permission List
* Scope Type

==================================================
5. DYNAMIC FILTER DROPDOWN
==========================

Saat pilih company:

* branch otomatis filter

Saat pilih branch:

* division otomatis filter

Saat pilih division:

* department otomatis filter

==================================================
6. PERMISSION FILTERING
=======================

Semua query wajib mengikuti scope role.

Contoh:

* Super Admin → semua company
* Company Admin → company sendiri
* Branch Manager → branch sendiri
* Division Manager → division sendiri
* Department Staff → department sendiri
